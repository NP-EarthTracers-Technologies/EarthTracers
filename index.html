AWESOME FEATURES

We are expertise in Geo-Spatial Data Processing Services, Photogrammetry, LiDAR, Mobile/UAV Processing, GIS Modeling and CAD & GIS Services

Geographical Information System (GIS)

Earthtracers can provide high-quality, low-cost GIS and desktop mapping services for a wide range of applications, including asset management, demographic analysis, environmental projects, agriculture, mineral exploration, mining production, and fire management.

We provide data generation, analysis, digitization, geospatial analysis, data auditing/cleansing, thematic mapping, and 3D data visualisation. We have extensive experience migrating CAD data to GIS environments as well as remediating low-quality engineering datasets.

Among our consulting services are the development and implementation of enterprise geospatial strategies, the integration of enterprise software with GIS software, and the deployment of web mapping systems that deliver information across an organisation, from customer service to remote field staff.

EARTHTRACERS have extensive experience documenting business requirements as well as assisting with geospatial project design, management, and delivery. We also provide a remote GIS bureau service for rural and remote local governments that find it hard to maintain a full-time professional role in their organisation.

Remote Sensing (RS)

We can analyse remotely sensed data to detect and model environmental health and vigour, operating temperature conditions, or volumetric calculations of stockpiles and quarries using a variety of technologies, whether satellite-based, airborne, or captured using our industrial survey grade drones.

LiDAR

LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a Remote Sensing Method that uses light in the from of a pulsed laser to measure ranges (Variable distances) to the Earth.

These light pulses-combined With other data recorded by the airborne system generate precise,three dimensional information about the shape of the earth and it's surface characteristics.

Airborne Laser Scanning (ALS).

Mobile Laser Scanning(MLS).

Digital Photogrammetry

The Earthtracers uses the Photogrammetry technique for the compilation of a real terrain into digital maps, this technique requires a processed output of Aerial images and analytical data's for providing a three dimensional data. This technique is mostly used to minimize the human errors and brings a accurate measurements for land survey.

Photogrammetry provides the elevation datasets like DEM points, Contours and DTM/DSM.

Also the processed output data is used for Urban development and Industry development. Since Urban development i.e., Structural as well as Innovative plans plays a vital role in Physical & economical status of a country with time to time.

Ocean Numerical Modelling

We handle various ocean numerical models such as TELEMAC, Mike21, ADCIRC+SWAN, WAM, GNOME, and DELFT3D for coastal and ocean studies, including shoreline management studies, EIA studies, pollution management plans, disaster management plans, storm surge studies, dispersion of brine waste discharges from a coastal desalination plant, hydrodynamics studies in the delta's nearshore and offshore regions, and coastal stability studies in the port area.

We are analysing oceanographic data and forecasting using soft computing approaches.

Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS or GPS)

When it comes to mobile mapping, it’s important that your systems are reliable and easy for your fieldworkers to use, without sacrificing project outcomes.

EARTHTRACERS was born in the field. Mobile mapping and GPS data capture are in our DNA so we understand the challenges of working outside in the heat and the cold, in the humid and wet conditions.

UAV Processing

UAVs or Drones have many uses, from simple Aerial Photography & Videography to more complex tasks such as Aerial Surveying & performing close proximity Asset Inspections.

UAV applications and services are only limited by the payloads. With EARTHTRACERS utilising a wide arrange of payloads from RGB, Multispectral & Thermal cameras, we are able to provide a large number of unique services to many industries.

Our recent project experience includes scenic imagery, pest and weed monitoring, 3D terrain modelling and precision horticulture. With a team that holds post-graduate qualifications in remote sensing and hyper-spectral imaging, we’re specialists in processing aerial data into information.
